# 🎓 VTOP+ — Reimagined Student Portal

A next-generation student portal that redefines the university experience with a modern interface, intelligent insights, and AI-powered assistance. VTOP+ centralizes every aspect of a student's academic and campus life—from academics and attendance to financial information, personal records, events, and extracurricular activities.
